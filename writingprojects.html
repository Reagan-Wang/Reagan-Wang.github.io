Umurangi Generation Narrative Analysis

In the first semester of my sophomore year at USC, I entered the Game Developers Conference's narrative analysis competition. I decided to write my analysis on Umurangi Generation, a game about the end of the world set in my home country of Aotearoa, New Zealand. For my efforts I was fortunate enough to be rewarded with a Gold Award, paying for my pass to GDC 2024 and continuing USC's streak of failing to produce any platinum award winners.
